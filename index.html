Flip - Flashcards Reinvented


Accelerate your language learning with Flip, a powerful flashcard app designed to optimize learning through time-spacing repetition.


App Store

Why Another Flashcard App?

For the past four years, I used Memrise (my profile) to learn French 🇫🇷, German 🇩🇪, and Italian 🇮🇹. But when support for user-created dictionaries came to an end, I decided to build Flip, focused purely on learning, without gimmicks or distractions.

Why Flip is Awesome?

Flip is built with the same core learning philosophy that made Memrise great, but structured in a way that keeps all your knowledge in one place rather than splitting it into multiple thematic sets.

🌍

30+ Languages Supported

Your language. Your choice. From Spanish and French to Japanese and Arabic, Flip supports over 30 languages to match your goals.

📚

Proofread Basic Set

Flip includes built-in basic dictionaries for popular languages, covering the most common words and phrases you need to get started.

🌟

Spaced Repetition Algorithm

Flip uses a proven spaced repetition system to show you cards just before you’re about to forget them – maximizing retention while minimizing study time.

📖

One Shared Dictionary

Flip keeps all words in one shared dictionary, not isolated sets, with automatic duplicate detection. Same word can never be added twice.

✍️

Effortless Word Adding

Just type the words you want to learn and Flip will find translations, format the cards, and prepare them for study. Prefer full control? Paste ready-made cards: word ; translation

🔊

Automatic Audio

Audio is pulled automatically for all cards, so your vocabulary is ready to learn right away.

🧘

Different Learning Methods 💅

Flip supports three effective learning methods to match your preferred style: choose the correct answer from six options, recall the translation from memory, or reverse the process by recalling the original word.

Word Explanation 💫

Understand nuances, compare similar words!

Not sure which word fits? Explanation compares similar words, explains the difference between answers with the same translation, and shows examples and usage notes.

Walking Mode🏃‍♂️‍➡️

Turn your daily walk into a lesson!

Walking Mode lets you repeat cards while you’re on the move – no need to stop or even look closely at the screen! Flip reads the question aloud. Tap once to reveal and hear the answer. Tap again to mark the card as remembered, or double tap if you forgot it.

Pay Only For Result

Flip is a results-based paid app. You’re not paying for your time or the app itself – only for the cards you master. If Flip doesn’t help you memorize a card, you don’t pay.

How does it work?

At the start, Flip gives you 100 Premium cards for FREE. When a card reaches Level 5 (after a 12-day successful repetition period), it begins to count toward your Premium balance. Once your balance runs out, you’ll need to top up to keep learning.

How many words do I need for daily conversations?

In general, knowledge of the most frequent 3,000 words in a language is sufficient to understand 85–90% of daily conversations, media, and written texts.
